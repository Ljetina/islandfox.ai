# Privacy Policy for IslandFox.ai

**Last Updated: 17-10-2023**

## 1. Introduction

Ljetina OÜ ("we", "us", or "our") operates IslandFox.ai. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information we receive from IslandFox.ai users.

## 2. Information Collection and Use

We collect IP, name, email and any additional personal information provided through the chat interface, to be able to provide the service to you. We do not use this data to improve our service directly, we rely on user feedback for this instead. We will only access your personal data for immediate support and only after requesting your explicit permission after you ask us for support.

## 3. Data Storage

User data is stored on an encrypted production database, shielded from public access, ensuring the highest level of security.

## 4. Data Sharing

Data is shared with a third-party processor, OpenAI, in order to provide the services of IslandFox.ai. We have a data processing agreement in place with OpenAI. Your data will not be used to train machine learning models. Please refer to this agreement for further details.

## 5. Cookies

We use "cookies" to collect information for site functionality and internal analytics.

## 6. User Rights

Users have the right to modify or delete their personal data. Most records can be deleted directly from the user interface, and users can delete their account from the settings page, which will consequentially wipe all personal data related to that account.

## 7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Any changes to this policy will be communicated to users via email.

## 8. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not intentionally gather personal information from visitors who are under the age of 16.


##### By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
